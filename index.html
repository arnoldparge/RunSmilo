How to play the game (Run Smilo):

  • Tilt(move or cause to move into a sloping position) your phone to move SMILO up, down, left and right
  • To score, collect as many donuts as you can by moving your smilo
  • DEVIL SMILO will always be running behind you to catch you
  • Speed of devil smilo will increase with time. So you have to get better
  • Increase your speed my tilting your phone more
  • Once the devil smilo catches you. GAME OVER

Inspired from:

I was inspired from an old, famous and our very favorite game - TAG. Tag, which is more commonly known as pakda - pakdi in India. I used to play tag when I was a kid. I miss that game a lot. Nowadays children are more into technology than natural and fun loving games. So I decided to bring back something like TAG. Run Smilo is not less excited than TAG. The excitement of running and not getting caught - WOW.
